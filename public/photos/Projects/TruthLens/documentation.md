## TruthLens - AI-Generated Content Detection Browser Extension

**Timeline:** March 2026

### Original Description

Every child deserves to see the world as it truly is, not as AI decides it should be.

Project Overview
Truthlens is a lightweight browser extension designed to protect digital reality for the next generation. While traditional parental controls focus on "what" kids see, we address how it was made—shielding families from the rising tide of synthetic deception.

Key Highlights

**Multi-Modal Detection:** Instantly identifies AI-generated video, images, and text across TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

**Seamless Protection:** Runs silently in the background with zero lag; no app-switching or manual reviews required.

**Parental Alerts:** Provides real-time notifications to parents, ensuring a unified safety net across every family device.

**The Competitive Edge:** Unlike Bark or Qustodio, which only filter keywords, Truthlens is the first to distinguish between real human content and deepfakes.

**The Mission:** To scale into a new "Cognitive Parental Control" market, ensuring children see the world as it truly is, not as AI decides it should be.

---

### Additional Context

**Differentiation:** Focuses on content authenticity verification rather than keyword filtering  
**Market Opportunity:** Emerging cognitive parental control category with deepfake technology advancing rapidly
