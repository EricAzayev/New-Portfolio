## GroupWisdom / WisdomBot - Discord Digital Twin Generator

### Original Description

🦜 Meet Parry the parrot. When he hears you "peekaboo!" or "I love integrals" (who says that over and over?) enough times, he will say right back at you!

AI Models follow a similar principle. With WisdomBot, I was able to prove that given enough conversations from a discord server, using the amazing tool of Unsloth, you can make it sound exactly like your friends!

Simply invite WisdomBot to your server of choice, and type /ForgeWisBot. After a few minutes, the bot with respond with "forged", indicating it's ready.

Now, type /@<user of choice>.

And Wisbot will respond to the last typed 2 or 3 messages using the last 6 as context. Your call to the bot is invisible to server!

While Parry might just be mimicking sounds, WisdomBot is performing high-fidelity pattern recognition. By architecting a pipeline that handles real-time Discord data ingestion, automated cleaning, and Unsloth-accelerated fine-tuning, I transformed raw social telemetry into a responsive, context-aware 'Digital Twin.' It turns a server's history into a functional, conversational system.

So next time Parry says "Anti derivatives are awesome", please note he's not being serious.

**Technology Stack:** Unsloth fine-tuning, Discord.py, LLM inference
